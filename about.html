Potter's Paint Studio started in 2018 out of Richmond, Virginia and is mostly run by two black cats and one chaotically creative lady.
We specialize in commission miniature figure painting, acryllic painting tutorials, and sculpture repaints.
Our figures are mostly used for board games and tabletop wargames like Dungeons & Dragons and Warhammer.
Feel free to contact us if you see something you like, dislike, or for any special requests!
